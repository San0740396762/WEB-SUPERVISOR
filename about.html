Our History

Established in 2016, Taveta Technical and Vocational College (Taveta TVC) was founded to provide technical and vocational training to the local community (Taveta). Starting with a modest enrollment of 50 students, the college has grown into a leading institution, equipping thousands of students with practical skills and knowledge.

Over the years, Taveta TVC has expanded its programs to meet the evolving needs of industries and communities. From its humble beginnings, the college has introduced modern facilities, diverse courses, and partnerships with key stakeholders to ensure students are prepared for the workforce.

In the 2020s, Taveta TVC embraced advancements in technology and innovation, integrating them into its curriculum to stay ahead in a rapidly changing world. The college also strengthened its focus on entrepreneurship and community development.

Today, Taveta TVC is recognized for its commitment to excellence in technical and vocational education, fostering innovation, and empowering students to contribute meaningfully to society and the economy.

Mission & Vision

Guiding our path forward with purpose and clarity

Our Mission

Taveta TVC is dedicated to providing holistic, quality, technical and vocational education and training grounded in practical skills, research, and Biblical Christian values.
Our mission drives us to:

  • Deliver accessible, high-quality education that equips students with practical skills and knowledge for a dynamic world
  • Engage in innovative research that addresses community and global needs
  • Champion diversity, equity, and inclusion as core principles of our institution
  • Empower communities through meaningful outreach, partnerships, and service
  • Inspire academic excellence, creativity, and a commitment to lifelong learning

Our Vision

Taveta TVC aspires to be a leading center of academic excellence in technical and vocational training and research, producing graduates with quality and relevant competency skills for self-reliance, industrialization, and global competitiveness.

  • Fostering academic excellence and innovative teaching methodologies
  • Conducting impactful research that addresses real-world challenges
  • Developing graduates who are critical thinkers, ethical leaders, and globally competitive professionals
  • Creating a collaborative and inclusive campus environment that values diversity
  • Promoting sustainable practices and responsible resource management
  • Building strong partnerships with industry, government, and community organizations

Campus Locations

Explore our beautiful campus-details around the world

Taveta Technical and Vocational College (TVC) is uniquely situated near the Taveta-Holili border crossing, making it a pivotal institution in the region. Its location not only facilitates accessibility for students from both Kenya and Tanzania but also positions it amidst spectacular surroundings that inspire and enhance the learning experience. The campus is enveloped by the tranquil beauty of Taveta's natural environment, providing a calm and refreshing atmosphere for academic and personal growth. With views of towering Mount Kilimanjaro in the distance and the serene Lake Chala within reach, Taveta TVC enjoys proximity to some of the most iconic landscapes in East Africa. The vibrant culture of the nearby town and the dynamic cross-border interactions further add to its charm, making the college a hub for diverse perspectives and opportunities.
