Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average GPA for admitted students?

The average GPA for admitted Intermidiate Vocational Qualification (level 3) is 3.7 on a 4.0 scale. However, we evaluate applications holistically, considering various factors beyond GPA.

Can I apply before receiving all my test scores?

Yes, you can submit your application before receiving all test scores. However, your application will not be reviewed until all required materials are received.

Is an interview required for admission?

Interviews are optional but recommended. They provide an opportunity for us to learn more about you and for you to ask questions about CampusVista University.

Can I defer my admission if accepted?

Yes, accepted students may request a deferral for up to one academic year. Deferral requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
